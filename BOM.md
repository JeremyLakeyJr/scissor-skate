## üß™ Material Options

### Filament Selection Guide

#### PLA
- **Cost**: $15-20/kg
- **Pros**: Easy to print, rigid, inexpensive
- **Cons**: Brittle, not heat resistant
- **Best For**: Prototyping, testing designs
- **Recommended**: For first prints to test fit

#### PETG
- **Cost**: $18-25/kg
- **Pros**: Durable, some flex, weather resistant
- **Cons**: Harder to print than PLA, strings
- **Best For**: Actual riding boards, general use
- **Recommended**: Default choice for functional board

#### Nylon
- **Cost**: $30-50/kg
- **Pros**: Very tough, impact resistant, flexible
- **Cons**: Difficult to print, absorbs moisture
- **Best For**: Heavy riders, aggressive riding
- **Recommended**: Experienced builders, high-stress parts

#### Carbon Fiber PETG
- **Cost**: $30-45/kg
- **Pros**: Strong, rigid, lightweight, premium feel
- **Cons**: Abrasive (use hardened nozzle), expensive
- **Best For**: Performance builds, weight reduction
- **Recommended**: Premium builds, structural parts

#### ASA
- **Cost**: $25-35/kg
- **Pros**: UV resistant, weather proof, strong
- **Cons**: Smelly when printing, needs enclosure
- **Best For**: Outdoor use, sun exposure
- **Recommended**: Boards stored outside
